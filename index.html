AISpark: Monster Survivors

Unleash your survival instincts in this AI-powered monster battle arena

Game Overview

AISpark: Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must battle against endless waves of AI-controlled monsters. Test your reflexes, strategy, and survival skills as you navigate through challenging terrains and face increasingly difficult enemies.

The game features adaptive AI that learns from your gameplay style, ensuring each session is unique and challenging. How long can you survive?

Key Features

  • • Advanced AI enemies that adapt to your gameplay
  • • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • • Procedurally generated levels for infinite replayability
  • • Upgrade system with over 50 different power-ups
  • • Global leaderboards to compete with players worldwide

How to Play

1

Select Your Character

Choose from multiple classes, each with unique strengths and abilities.

2

Defeat Monsters

Use WASD or arrow keys to move and fight against waves of intelligent monsters.

3

Upgrade & Survive

Collect power-ups and level up your abilities to survive longer.

Game Controls

Movement WASD or Arrow Keys
Special Ability Spacebar
Pause Game ESC or P
Skill Selection 1-4 Number Keys

System Requirements

Minimum: Any modern browser with WebGL support

Recommended: Chrome or Firefox with dedicated graphics

Mobile: iOS 14+ or Android 10+

*For the best experience, play on a desktop or laptop computer
